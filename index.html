DMU Table Tennis Posse

About Us

The Ping Pong Posse is DMU's famous collegiate table tennis team that competes in DONGBEI tournaments and other sanctioned competitions around China. Come on out to club nights (Mondays, Wednesdays from 6-8pm and Friday from 6-8pm at the Dongshan North Gym) if you are interested in competing for the team, practicing, learning, or just having a good time playing some table tennis. There are players of all skill levels, from recreational to professional. Join the Posse family!
